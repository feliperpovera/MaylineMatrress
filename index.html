About Maylin Mattress

Over fifty years of mattress craftsmanship in South Florida.

Maylin Mattress was founded more than fifty years ago and represents a family trade tradition passed down through three generations.

Our concept was the same then as it is now: make the best possible mattress at the best price in the market.

We do that by selling directly from our factory to the end consumer. When chain stores buy mattresses, they push for the lowest cost to maximize profit, and that usually forces manufacturers to cut quality.

Experience the best night's sleep ever. We guarantee it. If you don't, we will exchange it until you do. Make your mornings great again.
